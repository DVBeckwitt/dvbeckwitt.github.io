# Cover Letter Base Format

Use one page for industry roles. Use 1 to 1.5 pages only for academic teaching roles when the posting expects a formal job letter.

## Paragraph 1: role and fit

State the role and employer. Give David's current professional identity. Name the strongest match to the role and one employer-specific reason for interest.

## Paragraph 2: primary proof

Use one concrete example from the CV that maps to the core responsibility. Include methods and tools.

## Paragraph 3: complementary proof

Choose one secondary proof point: communication, teaching, leadership, software, instrumentation, outreach, or interdisciplinary work. For scientific sales, translate technical credibility into customer-facing value. For teaching, translate classroom methods into student-facing value.

## Paragraph 4: close

State the contribution David can make. Keep it direct. Do not restate the resume.

## Required checks

- Company name and role title are exact.
- No unsupported claims.
- No generic praise.
- No private phone/address unless approved.
- Ph.D. status remains expected May 2026 unless updated.
